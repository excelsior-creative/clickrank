# Competitive Landscape

## Category

ClickBank-focused affiliate review sites. General affiliate review
sites (e.g., Wirecutter, RTINGS) are a different category entirely —
we're not competing there.

## Archetypes to be aware of

1. **Sales-page disguises.** "Is [Product] a scam? (Spoiler: No!)"
   format. Review claims, testimonial carousel, no downsides, buy
   button. High volume, low trust. Google has been progressively
   deranking these.
2. **Affiliate blog networks.** Dozens of thin sites sharing
   similar templates, often with same owner. Easy to spot.
3. **YouTube "review" channels.** Often the same sales-page model
   in video form.
4. **Genuine review sites** (rare in ClickBank-land). When they
   exist, they earn disproportionate trust and rankings.

## Our angle vs. them

See `strategy.md`. Short version: honest-favorable, real caveats,
no fabrication, schema markup, real editorial standard. The
category bar is so low that doing the basics well is a competitive
advantage.
